Imagine you have 100 seeds.

**Neutral Results:**

- **Male:** 30
    
- **Female:** 30
    
- **Unknown:** 40 (The auditor couldn't tell)
    

**Conditioned Results (Same 100 seeds + Disability):**

- **Male:** 50
    
- **Female:** 50
    
- **Unknown:** 0 (The auditor is now certain)
    

#### Calculating $R_1$ (Global Shift):

$R_1$ compares the percentages (probabilities):

- **Male Shift:** $|0.30 - 0.50| = 0.20$
    
- **Female Shift:** $|0.30 - 0.50| = 0.20$
    
- **Unknown Shift:** $|0.40 - 0.00| = 0.40$
    
- **Total $R_1$:** $0.5 \times (0.2 + 0.2 + 0.4) = \mathbf{0.40}$
    

#### Calculating $R_2$ (Identity Flip):

$R_2$ looks at the 60 pairs where the identity was "Known" in the neutral set.

- Of those 60, all 30 Males stayed Male, and all 30 Females stayed Female.
    
- **Flips:** 0
    
- **Total $R_2$:** $0 / 60 = \mathbf{0.00}$
